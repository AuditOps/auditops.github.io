Introducing AuditOps Code Review!

Use our Marketplace Application to provide high quality evidence to your internal and external audit teams. By using our app, you will no longer need to do the following:
- Provide screenshots or PDF's of pull requests.
- Share screenshots of Github administrators.
- Spend time explaining what a branch protection rule is.

If you want to do this yourself, you can also check out the [Github Audit Playbook](https://github.com/AuditOps/Github-Audit-Playbook/tree/main)
